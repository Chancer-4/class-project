    Special Events announced monthly, and poted in Calender

Our Mission Statement

This club is dedicated to growing a healthy community of Magic: The Gathering players at Iowa State.
We seek to foster and cater to new and old players in a healthy environment.
Our mission is to promote casual and competitive card gaming in a positive, inclusive, and diverse community.
